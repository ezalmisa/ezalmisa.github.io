Project Summary

Halal Application Approval System (HAAS) is an online system that enables a Certification Body (CB) to manage their halal application processes from the beginning right up to the end.

The system is convenient to CBs as they can keep track and manage all applications via the online system.

From the same system, Halal Applicants (HA) are able to track the progress and status of their applications, any products nearing expiry dates and any pending documentation.

Project Goal

The main goal of this project is to come out with a solution help the certification bodies and halal applicants on the following business challenges:

Certification Bodies Problem

  • Unable to administer the application processes end to end effectively.
  • Unable to schedule audit and record findings collaboratively.
  • Unable to access records and clientsâ€™ information securely; anywhere and anytime.

Halal Applicants Problem

  • Unable to view the progress and status of the application online.
  • Unable to renew the applications without committing the full application process again.
  • Unable to keep track of historical records and information used for the earlier application.
